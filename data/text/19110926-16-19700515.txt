Immigrants from Denmark. | A communication has been received by the Minister for Lands from Count Sponneek, who writes from the Danish Consulate at Melbourne, offering to accept an appointment to secure imniigrante from Denmark for Queensland. The writer states that he is a . landholder of some standing in Denmark, and could secure 220 honest, thrifty, hard- working, and experienced farm labourers each year for this State. The communica- tion has been referred to the Chief Secre- tary.
