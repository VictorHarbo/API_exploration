MR. CALWELL'S TOUR OF EUROPE COPENHAGEN, July 11.-The Australian Minister for Immigration (Mr. Calwell) said today that he had made no agreement with Den mark for the migration of displaced persons to Australia because of Denmark's labour shortage. Mr. Calwell met representatives of the Danish Foreign Ministry, the Labour Ministry and the Emigra tion Committee and also the new Danish Commercial Attache for Sydney (Mr. F. Henning Hergel). He will leave today for Oslo.
