Naturalisation Ceremony CANBERRA, Wednesday.-Seven men of seven different nationalilties will become the first naturalised Australians under the new Nationality and Citizenship Act at a ceremony at Canberra tomorrow, The men are Spanish, Danish, French,, Yugoslav, Norwegian, Greek and Czech. During the ceremony, which will be performed by Mr. Justice Simpson, of the Canberra Supreme Court, the candidates will be welcomed by the Prime Minister (Mr. Chifley) and the Minister for Immigration (Mr Calwell). Other Cabinet Ministers, members of Parliament and service chiefs will also attend. The seven candidates include Paul Marvie, 54, a Dane, who came to Australia in 1916.
