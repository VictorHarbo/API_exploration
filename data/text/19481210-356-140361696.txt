Arranged Wedding to Speed Migration COPENHAGEN, Dec. 9. A.A.P. -Miss Gudrun Jensen, of Horsens (Jutland) will leave Copenhagen on December 18 on board the first Danish migrant plane for Australia to marry a farmer she has never seen. The bridegroom, Mr. George Seisor, of Northcliff (Western Australia), asked a neighbour who visited Denmark last summer to find him a housekeeper. The neighbour found Miss Jen sen and, to get the immigration formalities settled quickly, Mr. Seisor and Miss Jensen agreed -after an exchange of letters-to marry.
