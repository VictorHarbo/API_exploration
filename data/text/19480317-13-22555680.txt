Government will help allies to migrate here CANBERRA, Tues: Federal Cabi- net today approved of assisted pas- sages being granted to ^former fighting allies and members of the underground movements who fought the Axis powers during the war. The scheme will benefit Dutch, Belgian, French, Norwegian, and Danish nationals. Mr Calwell, Immigration Minister, said that ex-service personnel and their dependants would mainly bene- fit, but they would be subject to the usual immigration requirements and security checks.
