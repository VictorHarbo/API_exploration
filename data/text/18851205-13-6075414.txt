SPECIAL TELEGRAMS. (By Cable From: Cent Conn_srO-DB"ST.) DANISH IMMIGRANTS FOR QUEENSLAND,. LONDON, Dec. 3. Mr. J. F. Garrick, the agent-general for Queensland, who recently went to Copenhagen in connexion with the proposed despatch of Danish emigrants to that colony, has been informed that the Danish Government is willing to furnish the colony with a supply of indented labourers. THE STEAMSHIP JOHN ELDER. LONDON, Dec. 3 Captain Cooper, of the Orient steamer John Elder, has denied the report that the vessel had a miraculous escape' on Sunday last of being wrecked on. the rocks off the island of Ushant, on the west coast of France.
