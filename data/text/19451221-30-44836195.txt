TO STUDY AUSTRALIA. European Journalists' Visits. SYDNEY, Dec 20.-Six Danish and six Norwegian journalists will visit Australia to report on condi tions here for the benefit of prospec tive migrants, said Mr L. Haylen, M.H.R., today. Mr Haylen recently returned from abroad after making a study of immigration questions. He said that arrangements for the visits had been made by the Minis ter for Immigration (Mr Calwell). The tours of the journalists would be "staggered" so that they would not all arrive together
