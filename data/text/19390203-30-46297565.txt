FREMANTLE CHILDREN'S COURT. (Thursday.-Before Mr. A. Schroeder. S.ML.) Deserter from Ship.-A 15-year-old Danish ship's boy. who deserted from the Danish i motorship Westralia on January 15, appeared on a charge of being a prohibited immigrant, to which he had previously pleaded guilty. He was released in the custody of the Rev. J. W. Clift. of the Fremantle Missions to Seamen, to be placed in the Swan Boys' Orphanage until arrangements could be made for him to be placed on a ship returning to Denmark.
