Yesterday's Messages. DiNISH SETTLEBS. TOWNSVILLE, Friday.-Mr. Petersen, one of tbo delegates to Brisbane from the recent Sugar Conference, while in Brisbane last week, bad an interview with the Pre mier on the subject of a letter from the Scandinavian Government enquiring if Queensland were suitable for the settlement of Danish immigrants. The Premier evinced considerable in terest in the matter, and expressed the opinion tbat the Scandinavians were some of the best colonists in Queensland. The Premier passed the matter on to the Agricultural Department.
