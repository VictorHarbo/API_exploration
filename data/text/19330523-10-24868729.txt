I TO LEAVE AUSTRALIA DEPORTATION OF FOREIGNERS. BRISBANE, May 22. Four foreigners-two Italians, a Ger- man, and a Dane-are to be deported under the Immigration Act. One of the Italians was convicted and sentenced to Imprisonment ' for six months for abduction of a girl near Ingham recently. Tho other Italian Is In an asylum. Both will be deported to Italy by the Italian motor ship Rom olo, which will leave Brisbane on Satur- day. The German deserted from the German vessel Strassfurt at Brisbane on April 28, and will be deported to Ger- many by the steamer Alster on Satur- day. The Dane deserted from the Danish motor ship Columbia at Gladstone, and was sentenced to six montths in gaol pending deportation.
