Picture of the month "DOLD blues and greens highlighted the seascape by Swedish artist Mrs. Solveig Douglas, that was displayed during April at the Federal Immigration Department's offices at 18-20 York Street, Sydney. Mrs. Douglas, then Solveig Blomquist, arrived in Australia in October, 1951\. She married Australian John Douglas soon after. Solveig Douglas studied art under well-known Swedish artist Iage Hansson and Danish artists Clemmesen and Kirsten Gye Sejg. The picture displayed, FROM MY WINDOW, was also shown at the Contemporary Art Society's recent exhibition in Sydney.
