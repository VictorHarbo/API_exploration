MIGRANTS TO BE NATURALISED Seven men of seven different nationalities will become the first naturalised Australians under the Nationality and Citizenship Act at a ceremony at Canberra to-day. The men are Spanish, Danish, French, Yugoslav, Norwegian, Greek and-Czech. During the ceremony, which will be performed by Mr. Justice Simpson, of the Canberra Supreme Court, the candidates will be welcomed by the Prime Minister (Mr. Chifley) and the Immigration Minister (Mr. Calwell). Other Cabinet Ministers, members of Parliament and Service Chiefs will also attend.
