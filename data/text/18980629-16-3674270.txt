VICE-REGAL levee. CORONATION DAY ANNIVERSARY?. His Excellency the Governor held a levee at Government House yesterday morning in honour of the sixty-first anniversary of the Coronation Day of her Majesty the Queen. A detachment of the Moreton Regiment, under Captain Turner, was present as a guard of honour. The attendance was numerous, but was not so large as is usually witnessed at the Birthday levees.
