Naturalisation Ceremony At Canberra Today CANBERRA, Wed.-Seven men of different nationalities will become the first naturalised Australians under the new Nationality and Citizenship Act at a ceremony at Canberra tomorrow. The men are Spanish, Danish, French, Yugoslav, Norwegian, Greek, and Czechoslovak. During the ceremony which will be performed by Mr. Justice Simp- son of . the Canberra Supreme Court, candidates will be welcomed by the Prime Minister (Mr. Chifley) and the Minister for Immigration (Mi'. Calwell). Other Cabinet Ministers, members of Parliament, and service chiefs also will attend. The seven candidates will include from Tasmania Thiel Matstrand (50), a Norwegian, who reached Australia in 1945 after having been interned by the Japanese in Malaya He is a timber mill manager.
