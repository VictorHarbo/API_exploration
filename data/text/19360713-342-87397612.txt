TWO FOREIGN SEAHEN ADMITTED FEDERAL CHARGES WITHDRAWN Adelaide, July 10. When two foreign seamen were called before Mr. Sanderson, S.M., in the IPort Adelaide Police Court to ansrwer charges that they were prohibited immigrants because they failed to pass the dictation tests, it was announced that the Minister far Customs, Mr. White, had decided that the men were desirable types of migrants. As a result, the prose cution was withdrawn. The men were Franz Rarcz marczyk, a German national, and = Frederick William Kruse, a Danish national.
