Danes Leaving Home COPENHAGEN (A.P.). — More and more Danes want to leave their native country and settle down as Immigrants in othpr continents. America is generally preferred. The Danish state runs a migra tion office assisting everybody who prepares to leave the country. Nine hundred left Denmark in 1947\. 770 went to the United States of America, the rest to British Dominions. This year 1000 Danes have mi grated so far. 650 going to the U.S.A., 200 to Canada, 54 to Australia, 26 to New Zealand and the rest to other parts of the world.
