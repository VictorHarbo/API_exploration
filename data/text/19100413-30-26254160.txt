AUSTRALIAN IMMIGRATION.I THE DANISH WARNING. London, April 11. The Danish Miniter in London has explained to Mr T. A. Coghlan (Agent General for New South Wales) that his' recent warning to emigrants against going to Sydney from. Denmark was made on what he considered to be suffi cient authority. The Danish'Consuls in Australia had been instructed to-report upon the matter, and if the statements originally made were found to be untrue or exaggerated the Foreign Ofice at Covenhagen wiuld issue the necessary correction.
