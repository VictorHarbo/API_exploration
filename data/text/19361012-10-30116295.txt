DANISH IMMIGRANTS Possibility of Settlement in Australia PERTH, October ll. The possibility ot abandoned and other holdings on group settlements in the south-west being taken over by Danish immigrants is being closely considered by the Consul-General for Denmark (Mr. G. L. Hoest), who has returned from a visit to the groups to j investigate possible openings for Danes. Danish diplomatic and consular representatives, he said, had been asked to study the migration position in the countries in which they lived. In view of the talk about immigration it was thought that Danish people might be allowed to settle in Australia.
