WAR WIZARD DEAD Detroit, April 28.-William , S. Knudsen, next most important man to President Roosevelt in U.S. dur- ing the war, and former president of General Motors- Corporation, has died. Mr. Knudsen - born Signius Wilhelm Poul Knudsen - came to U.S. on a Danish immigrant ship when aged 20. Thereafter his career was the classical example of the srelf-made man.
