A DENMARK RESPONSE THE Markovitzes — the only Jewish family in Bucks County, Pennsylvania — placed a menorah in their window during Chanukah. The window and the menorah were smashed. Three high school students were later charged. But before that happened JTA reports that about two dozen, mostly Christian, neighbours went out and, with considerable difficul ty, found menorahs to put in their own windows as a mark of solidar ity with the Markovitzes, who are recent Russian immigrants.
