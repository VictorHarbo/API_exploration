DANISH EDITOR ON WORLD TOUR Mr. Leif B. Hendil, Chief Editor of Ekstrabladet, the larg- est evening newspaper in Copen- hagen, visited Canberra, yester- day in the course of a world tour. Ekstrabladet was commenced during the Russo-Japanese war and recently celebrated its jubi- lee. While in Canberra, Mr. Hendil conferred with the Department of Immigration and was an in- terested visitor to the National Library and the Australian Na- tional University.
