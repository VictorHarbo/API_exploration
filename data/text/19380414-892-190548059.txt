Attempted Assassination? Shots Fired In Danish Parliament COPENHAGEN, Wednesday. — A young man made an unsuccessful attempt on the life of the Minister for Justice (M. Steincke). He fired several shots in Parliament, but the Minister was not injured. The • attempt occurred while Parlia ment <was (debating the new Immigra tion Bill, to which the Danish Nahs are opposed. The young man who fired -the shots was sitting in the visitors' gallery with a number of others, who, it is believed, were Nazis. All were arrested; Later.—Inquiries subsequently indi cated that the shots were not fired with the intention of- harming anyone, but to draw attention to the Nazi leaflets which .the demonstrator, Erik Wester gaard, showered down, accusing Parlia ment of corruption and misgovernment.
